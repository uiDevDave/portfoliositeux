The Gym Group App

Business Requirements

Review goal of product/service:

  • Members to track fitness record
  • Members to sign up to gym classes
  • Members to be up to date with gym news e.g. refurbishment, times etc.
  • Members to engage with one another
  • Members to engage with PT
  • Members only but! new customers can register to join The Gym

App features:

  • Class and gym info
  • Class registration
  • Add, improve, view and monitor personal fitness
  • Profile area
  • Add members/friend, invite friends and share information
  • Messaging service
  • Gym usage

Unique Selling Points:

  • Members in class, when they engaging with one another during the session they get to know each other but may not feel comfortable in exchange numbers but can add each other on the app social network and stay motivated and in touch for the next classes
  • Members can check LIVE how busy the gym is
  • Members can set goals for how many times to visit gym per week and the app can monitor their attendance if any outstanding the app will remind/motivate them. App can track gym visiting patterns.
