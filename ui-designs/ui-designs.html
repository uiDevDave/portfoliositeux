The App

First app launch

Current menbers can downlaod The Gym Group app on their mobile devices to start using and for new member they can still download the app but will be directed to the The Gym Group website to sign up and then they can start using the app.

Login

Members login area

For new and current members, here you see a simple login form and after members has logged in first time they will see a series of onboarding messages to gather personal details and set goals. The purpose of this is to collect members details e.g. weight, height etc and also for members to set goals to achieve e.g. how many times they want to go gym per week, daily steps count, various training activities to be monitored and tracked.

Profile

Members info

Every member will have their own profile area where they can make updates and change their details such as weight, display picture etc. Members can see their status on their activity level, classes etc.

Fitness tracker

Record & keep track

This is where members can see their daily fitness level. The goals set by members can be tracked to help motivated them to completion day by day. Here members can also check gym usage, lists of current goals they have set for them selves, recent classes taken and many more.

Book Class

Booking process

This will be a cool way for members to easily book classes on the go, simple because everyone has a mobile phone with them 24/7 and booking on the go will be simple and convenient. This will create easy access and will not only restrict members to booking through the next available PC (website) or onsite at The Gym.

A simple booking step:

  1. List of location doing classes which they can choose
  2. Select from a list of dates available
  3. Select from a list of classes and check available spaces left
  4. Simple confirm by clicking the book now button link
